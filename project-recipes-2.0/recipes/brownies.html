Brownies

A simple recipe to make if you want something chocolaty

Brownies are often served as a dessert accompanied with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream, but they are actually born as a dessert on the go, practical and easy to carry.
And I reccomand to do it! It's beginner-freindly to make so it's impossible to go wrong. Also you can even add and change the ingredients if you like different type of chocolate or if you like to have some consistency(I reccomand to add 100gr of nuts).

Ingredients:

  • 200 gr dark chocolate
  • 90 gr 00 flour
  • 90 gr sugar
  • 100gr butter
  • 20 gr cocoa
  • 1 pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Break the chocolate into small pieces(by hand or by the help of the knif) and melt the chocolate in a vertical pot and add also the butter together. If you don't want to use the stove, you can also put the chocolate and the butter on a bowl and microwave it. But be careful! Remember to stir every 30sec if you decide to use this method.

  3. Combine the two compounds and here you can add whatever you want(nuts, chocolate chips...) and mix well.
